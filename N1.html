Recommended Diet Plan for old age people

  1. Healthy Eating for Older Adults.
  2. Eat fruits and vegetables.
  3. Vary protein choices with more fish, beans and peas.
  4. Eat at least three ounces of whole-grain cereals, breads, crackers, rice or pasta every day.
  5. Have three servings of low-fat or fat-free dairy (milk, yogurt or cheese) that are fortified with vitamin D to help keep your bones healthy.
